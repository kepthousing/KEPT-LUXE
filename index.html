Shipping Information

How We Ship

Every KEPT LUXE® order is carefully packed and shipped by us personally. Your pieces are wrapped in tissue, placed in a branded box, and sealed with care before they leave our hands. We ship via USPS and UPS.

Processing & Delivery

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of purchase.
  • Standard shipping: 3–5 business days after processing.
  • Expedited shipping: 2 business days after processing, where available.
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the following business day.

Tracking Your Order

A tracking number will be sent to you as soon as your order ships. You can follow your package directly through Amazon or the carrier's website. If you have not received tracking information within 3 business days of placing your order, please reach out to us at customersupport@keptluxe.com and we will look into it right away.

Packaging

Every KEPT LUXE® order arrives in a matte black box with white tissue, a branded card, and a ribbon or wax seal. The unboxing is part of the experience — because you deserve that from the very first moment it arrives at your door.

We take every precaution to ensure your order arrives in perfect condition. Should your package experience any damage in transit, we sincerely apologize in advance. Please photograph both the packaging and the item upon delivery and reach out to us at customersupport@keptluxe.com — we will take care of you.

Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

We stand behind every piece in the KEPT LUXE® collection. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we gladly accept returns within 30 days of your delivery date.

Your first return on any order is complimentary. Should you need to return an additional item from the same order, return shipping will be the responsibility of the customer.

Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following:

  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Unworn, unused, and in its original condition with all packaging intact.
  • Free of any odors, perfumes, or scents of any kind.
  • Free of any signs of wear, use, or handling beyond initial inspection.
  • Free of any damage, stains, marks, or alterations.
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase — your Amazon order number.
Any garment or item that arrives showing signs of wear, fragrance, damage, or use will be unable to be accepted and will be returned to you. We appreciate your grace in understanding — our standards exist to protect the integrity of every piece that carries the KEPT LUXE® name.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Email customersupport@keptluxe.com with your Amazon order number and reason for return.
  • We will review your request and respond within 2 business days.
  • If approved, you will receive return instructions. Your first return includes a complimentary prepaid label.
  • Once your return is received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or you received the wrong item, please photograph both the item and the packaging immediately upon delivery and contact us within 48 hours at customersupport@keptluxe.com. We will make it right — that is our promise to you.

Repair Program

Your piece is an investment.

At KEPT LUXE®, we believe luxury should endure. Every piece we offer is chosen for quality and longevity — and we stand behind that. If your item ever needs attention, we want to help you restore it to its original standard.

Our repair program exists to extend the life of your KEPT LUXE® pieces — not replace them. Because something worth having is worth caring for.

What We Repair

  • Loose or broken stitching on scarves, bags, and garments
  • Damaged hardware on bags and accessories
  • Hat reshaping and brim restoration
  • Sunglass frame adjustments and screw replacement
  • General restoration and care guidance

Pricing

We are thoughtfully finalizing our repair pricing to ensure it remains as accessible as it is worthy of the work involved.

Minor repairs — An assessment is required. Please reach out for a personalized quote.

Major repairs or restoration — Reviewed individually. A quote is always shared before any work begins. You will never be charged without your approval.

How to Submit a Repair Request

  • Email customersupport@keptluxe.com with the subject line: Repair Request — [Item Name].
  • Include your original order number and clear photographs of the item and the area needing care.
  • We will respond within 3–5 business days with a thoughtful assessment and next steps.
  • Once approved, we will provide a return address for your item.
  • After repair, your piece will be returned in KEPT LUXE® packaging — because the experience matters, even the second time around.
