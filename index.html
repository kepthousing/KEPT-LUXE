Our Story

The Beginning

A clothing and accessories brand built for those who demand the extraordinary.

KEPT LUXE® was created with a singular vision — to build a fashion brand where clothing and accessories exist in perfect harmony. Every collection is designed with intention: garments and pieces that speak to each other, complement each other, and elevate whoever wears them.

We began with accessories because detail is where identity starts. A silk scarf. A wide-brim hat. A summer tote. The pieces that complete a look and tell the world exactly who you are before you say a word. Clothing follows — and when it does, it will be worthy of everything that came before it.

The Name

What does it mean to Stay Kept?

To be kept is not to be contained. It means to be preserved. To be maintained at the highest standard. Every piece in the KEPT LUXE® collection — every garment, every accessory — is made to last, made to matter, and made to move with you.

Luxe is not a price point. It is a standard. The refusal to settle — in what you wear, in what you carry, in how you show up. Stay Kept.

Shipping Information

How We Ship

Every KEPT LUXE® order is packed and shipped by us personally. Your pieces are wrapped in tissue, placed in a branded box, and sealed with care before they ever leave our hands. We ship via USPS and UPS.

Processing & Delivery

  • Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of purchase.
  • Standard shipping: 3–5 business days after processing.
  • Expedited shipping: 2 business days after processing (where available).
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

Tracking Your Order

A tracking number will be emailed to you as soon as your order ships. You can track your package directly through Amazon or the carrier's website. If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days of your order, please reach out to us at customersupport@keptluxe.com and we will look into it right away.

Packaging

Every KEPT LUXE® order arrives in a matte black box with white tissue, a branded card, and a ribbon or wax seal. The unboxing is part of the experience — because you deserve that from the moment it arrives at your door.

We take every precaution to ensure your order arrives in perfect condition. However, if your package arrives damaged during transit, we sincerely apologize in advance. Please photograph the packaging and item immediately upon delivery and reach out to us at customersupport@keptluxe.com — we will make it right.

Returns & Exchanges

Our Return Policy

We stand behind every piece in the KEPT LUXE® collection. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 30 days of your delivery date.

Your first return on any order is free. Any subsequent returns from the same order are the responsibility of the customer and will require a prepaid return label purchased by the customer.

Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet all of the following:

  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date — no exceptions.
  • Unworn, unused, and in its original condition with all packaging intact.
  • Free of any odors, perfumes, scents, or smells of any kind.
  • Free of any signs of wear, use, or handling beyond initial inspection.
  • Free of any damage, stains, marks, or alterations.
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (Amazon order number).
Please note: Any garment or item that arrives showing signs of wear, odor, damage, or use will not be accepted and will be returned to the customer. We appreciate your understanding — our standards exist to protect every customer who wears a KEPT LUXE® piece.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items returned after the 30-day window.
  • Items showing any signs of wear, use, washing, or alteration.
  • Items with any odor including perfume, smoke, or food.
  • Items damaged after delivery without photographic evidence submitted within 48 hours.
  • Final sale items marked as such at the time of purchase.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Email customersupport@keptluxe.com with your Amazon order number and reason for return.
  • We will review and respond within 2 business days.
  • If approved, you will receive return instructions. Your first return includes a prepaid label.
  • Once received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please photograph the item and packaging immediately and contact us within 48 hours at customersupport@keptluxe.com. We will make it right — that is our promise.

Repair Program

Your Piece is an Investment.

At KEPT LUXE®, we believe luxury should last. Every piece we offer — every accessory, every garment — is chosen for quality and longevity. And we back that up.

If your item ever needs attention, we want to help you restore it. Our repair program is designed to extend the life of your KEPT LUXE® pieces rather than replace them.

What We Repair

  • Loose or broken stitching on scarves, bags, and garments
  • Damaged hardware on bags and accessories
  • Hat reshaping and brim restoration
  • Sunglass frame adjustments and screw replacement
  • General restoration and care guidance

Pricing

We are finalizing our repair pricing to ensure it stays accessible while reflecting the quality of care each piece receives.

Minor repairs — Assessment required. Contact us for a quote.

Major repairs or restoration — Reviewed case by case. A quote is always provided before any work begins. You are never charged without your approval.

How to Submit a Repair Request

  • Email customersupport@keptluxe.com with the subject line: Repair Request — [Item Name].
  • Include your original order number and clear photos of the item and the area needing repair.
  • We will respond within 3–5 business days with an assessment and next steps.
  • Once approved, we will provide a return shipping address.
  • After repair, your item is returned in KEPT LUXE® packaging — because the experience matters, even the second time around.
